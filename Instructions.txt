The Sequence Calculator is an app offering the facility to calculate sequences.

Calculate a sequence is very easy:
1 - Fill the input with a valid positive integer number.
2 - Press enter or click on submit.

That's it! The page will be rendered with 5 sequences: All numbers, Even number, Odd number, Replaced list and Fibonacci list.
Ah! You can also clear the data on scree just clicking on "Clear" link.
